Time Bell Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026. 01.03

1. Information We Collect

Time Bell does not collect any personal information from users.

All schedule information configured within the app is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Permissions Used

Time Bell requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Do Not Disturb Permission (ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY): Used to switch between silent/vibration modes.
  • Battery Optimization Exception (REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS): Required for accurate schedule execution.
  • Exact Alarm Permission (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): Necessary to maintain scheduled times precisely.
  • Permission to change audio settings (MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS): Used to change the ringtone mode.

All permissions are essential to provide the app's core functionality (automatic change of schedule-based ringtone mode).

3. Data Storage

  • All data, including your schedules and settings, is stored locally on your device.
  • Do not transfer data to an external server.
  • Do not share data with third parties.
  • When you delete the app, all stored data will be deleted together.

4. Third-Party Services

Time Bell does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app covers all age groups and does not intentionally collect children's personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the “Last Updated” date.
